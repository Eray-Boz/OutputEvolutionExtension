# Privacy Policy for OutputEvolution

**Last Updated: February 8, 2026**

## Overview

OutputEvolution is a browser extension that enhances the visual appearance of ChatGPT and Gemini outputs. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**OutputEvolution does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### What we store locally:
- **User preferences**: Your theme selection and intensity settings are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's `storage.sync` API.

### What we DO NOT collect:
- ❌ Personal information
- ❌ Browsing history
- ❌ Conversation content
- ❌ Authentication credentials
- ❌ Cookies or tracking data
- ❌ Analytics or usage statistics

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Why We Need It |
|------------|----------------|
| `storage` | To save your preferences (theme, intensity) locally |
| `activeTab` | To apply styles to the current page when you activate the extension |
| `host_permissions` | To inject styling CSS into ChatGPT and Gemini |

## Third-Party Services

OutputEvolution does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or data collection services.

The Quicksand font is loaded from **Google Fonts** only within the extension's own popup window. No external resources are loaded on ChatGPT or Gemini pages.

## Data Security

All your settings are stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in secure storage. No data leaves your browser.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
